# Sponsors

bpfcompat thanks the individuals and organizations that fund or provide
material infrastructure for its independent compatibility work.

This public ledger starts in July 2026. Project-specific supporters will be
listed here after funding or infrastructure is confirmed and recognition
permission is recorded.

## Financial sponsors

No project-specific financial sponsors are currently listed.

## Infrastructure and service sponsors

No project-specific infrastructure sponsors are currently listed.

## Recognition policy

- Listing is optional and requires the sponsor's permission.
- Names and logos acknowledge support; they do not imply project endorsement.
- Ended sponsorships remain in a dated past-supporters section unless removal
  is requested.
- Sponsorship never changes compatibility verdicts, governance rights, or
  security-disclosure access.

To support the project, see [FUNDING.md](FUNDING.md) or
[GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/ErenAri).
